## Reading

- 18 "4-of-4" domains are the priority outreach list. Almost every editorial PM-tool roundup in the English-language web is on this list.
- The reason Linear isn't on these roundups isn't authority (Linear's DA is competitive) — it's that the listicle editors are unaware Linear has grown beyond "Jira-for-engineers" into mainstream PM positioning. The outreach angle is education, not horse-trading.
- Forbes, PCMag, TechRadar, ZDNet rotate vendors slowly (1-2 changes per refresh, every 6-12 months). Pitching these takes patience but the placement compounds.
