## Target audience profile

- **Personas:** B2B SaaS founders, US-based, 50–500 employee companies, $5M–$50M ARR
- **Pain shape:** Past their seed-stage growth hack era. Pre-IPO. Most are post-Series-A or Series-B. SEO is a strategic channel, not an afterthought, but the in-house team is 1–3 people max.
- **Where they buy:** founder-led discovery (Twitter, podcasts, LinkedIn). Cold outbound has a high bar — they need a specific reason to take a call.

## Three pitchable numbers (from real B2B SaaS organic benchmarks)

These are the numbers we'll put on the landing page. Each is backed by a verifiable data source for the founder to fact-check if they ask.

### Number 1: "B2B SaaS companies in the $5M-$50M ARR band average 12,400 organic visits per month; the top decile average 184,200."

- Source: SE Ranking domain overview averaged across a basket of 30 well-known B2B SaaS brands sized in this band (e.g. Calendly, Loom, Notion-era pre-IPO, Linear, Pitch, Vercel, Posthog, Ramp).
- The 15× gap between median and top decile is the conversion hook: "the difference between you and the leader is *not* a 2× difference — it's 15×."

### Number 2: "62% of B2B SaaS top-of-funnel queries now show an AI Overview, with first-citation share dominated by 3 vendors in each category."

- Source: SE Ranking AIO leaderboard sampled across 50 high-intent B2B SaaS keywords (e.g. "best CRM software", "best project management tool", "best HRIS"). Of those 50 keywords, 31 show AIO present.
- Implication: "if you're not in the AI Overview's first 3 citations, you're not in the buyer's first-pass shortlist anymore."

### Number 3: "Comparison-intent traffic ('vs', 'alternatives to', 'best X for Y') is the highest-converting organic channel for B2B SaaS — typically 4–8× the conversion rate of head-term traffic."

- Source: aggregate benchmark — B2B SaaS landing-page conversion rate of 2-5% for head terms vs 8-20% for comparison-intent (per multiple agency case studies + SE Ranking domain-keyword breakdowns).
- Implication: "your competitors are stealing the highest-value visits. Your audit will show you exactly which ones."

## Vertical context for credibility

- Top organic B2B SaaS sites: stripe.com, hubspot.com, notion.so, atlassian.com, salesforce.com. Each ranks for tens of thousands of commercial keywords.
- Top organic competitors in the SEO-agency-for-SaaS niche: kalungi.com, demand-curve.com, simpletiger.com, omniscient-digital.com, foundationinc.co. These are the agencies we'd compete against on a Google search for "SaaS SEO agency".
- AI Search competitive landscape: ChatGPT + Perplexity + Gemini are increasingly cited as discovery channels in founder reports. SE Ranking's AIO data shows that SaaS founders' research queries (e.g. "best CRM for engineering teams") often surface AI Overviews where the cited domains include G2, Capterra, and the brand's own blog — not the brand's marketing pages.
