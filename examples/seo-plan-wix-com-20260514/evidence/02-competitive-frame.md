## What this tells the plan

- Wix is NOT competing with Wordpress, Shopify, or Framer head-on — those are different categories.
- The competitive frame is the website-builder cluster: 5 domains, Wix in front.
- The cluster's weakest member (Webflow) is the one Wix should target with Phase 2 — Webflow's small footprint is concentrated in agency / designer queries, which is exactly the segment Wix Studio is positioned for.
- Squarespace is the only competitor where ChatGPT specifically is close — flag for Phase 3 monitoring but not a Phase 2 priority because the SoV is held by AI Overview where Wix's lead is largest.
